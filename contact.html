Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with ChartNOW? ▼
Simply sign up for a free account, upload your CSV file, and our AI will suggest the best charts. No credit card required for the free plan.
What file formats do you support? ▼
We support CSV, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), JSON, and Google Sheets integration. Up to 100MB file size on paid plans.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer team or enterprise plans? ▼
Yes! Contact our business team at business@chartnow.com for custom enterprise solutions with advanced features and priority support.
