Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Free Trial and Free Plan?
Free Trial lets you create single charts without an account but you can't save them. Free Plan requires an account and gives you full dashboard access with saving capabilities.
Can I upgrade from Free Trial to Free Plan?
Yes! Simply create an account and all your trial charts will be saved to your new account automatically.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.
Is there a free trial for the Pro Plan?
Yes! All Pro Plan features are available for 14 days with no credit card required.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time and continue using the Free Plan features.
